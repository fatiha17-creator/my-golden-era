My One and Only

When I was 20s...
I was wondering—
Who would wanna spend his life with me, who would accept me, who wanna build a future together, chase our dreams, face problems side by side, and stay when things get hard?

He really came to my world,
bringing his light, his love.
We hold our hands tight.
We liked each other.
We fell in love.

We made our days feel like two birds,
flying high—seeing the world,
painting our lives with colors on the clouds.

Thank you for loving me.
I’ve become the luckiest person in the world.
